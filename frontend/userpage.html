CREDIT CARD PROCESSING

The end product is a web-app used to process and manage real-time card usage and payments.

Scope

The Software Requirements Specification captures all the requirements in a single document. The Credit Card Processing that is to be developed allows the banks and customers to interact via the software and make the various tasks easier than the usual way.

What problems do we solve?

Traditional method of applying for a new card was to physically go to a bank, fill up a form and wait for a few days. Any query related to the usage also had to be resolved physically with the help of a bank manager. This process can be digitized with the use of our app.

Account Details

Customers can get information about their account details.

Online Payment

Customers can make online transactions.

Credit / Debit Card

We provide fast and secure credit/debit card creation in online mode.

Our Services

Track Usage

The user can track the amount of and number of times the card has been used. The usage of card can be tracked in form of table as well as in form of charts and graphs.

Create Card

This feature allows the user to apply for a new card for online and offline usage. The card has options for international usage as well.

Card Payment

The outstanding amount on the card can be paid for the card with any net banking or UPI app. The remaining balance and credit limit on a specific card is also visible.

Learn More

Reward Points

Depending on the spending limit set on the card and amount spent using the card, the user is awarded reward points on that card. The account holder can then redeem these reward points for buying coupons for different brands.

Learn More

Deregister Card

The user can holster the card and deactivate the card for further use.
